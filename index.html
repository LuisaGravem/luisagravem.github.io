Claim

The black box does not contain a main tube that has two holes (that connect to another tube) and an output tube connected to the second tube

Evidence

Initially, the data we collected from the box showed that different amounts of water came out of the box with no regard to the amount of water inputted

My group's approach was to create a water reserve with two points the water could empty out

depending on the amount of water in the box. Our results ended up being the opposite of the initial results

For example, when we input 50 mL, 25 mL consistently comes out

Further, each output was less than the input.
